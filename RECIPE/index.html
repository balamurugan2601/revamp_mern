Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins | Serves: 6

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Beat in wet ingredients.
  4. Pour into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 mins or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool and serve.
